1. Introduction

The Nigeria Hydrological Services Agency ("NIHSA", "we", "our") operates NIHSA FloodWatch, an official government mobile application providing real-time flood monitoring, early warning alerts, hydrological data, and community flood reporting for citizens of Nigeria. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver real-time flood alerts, river gauge readings, and hydrological forecasts relevant to your location
  • Verify and publish community flood reports for public safety
  • Enforce per-role daily limits on the NIHSA AI assistant to prevent abuse
  • Send password reset codes to your registered email address
  • Compile anonymous, aggregated statistics for national flood intelligence reports
  • Comply with Nigerian federal law and court orders

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Other Federal Agencies — Nigeria Meteorological Agency (NiMet), National Emergency Management Agency (NEMA), State Emergency Management Agencies (SEMAs) — for coordinated disaster response
  • Service Providers — cloud hosting (Render.com), email delivery (Resend) — under strict data processing agreements
  • Law Enforcement — when required by Nigerian law or valid court order

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active + 2 years after deletion request
  • Flood reports: retained indefinitely as part of the national hydrological record
  • AI query logs: 90 days rolling window
  • Password reset OTPs: deleted immediately after use or after 15-minute expiry

6. Security

We implement industry-standard protections including bcrypt password hashing, JWT authentication, HTTPS-only communication, and server-side API key management. No API keys or sensitive credentials are ever transmitted to or stored on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

NIHSA FloodWatch is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has registered, contact us at info@nihsa.gov.ng and we will delete the account.

8. Your Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Object to processing for non-essential purposes
  • Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)

To exercise these rights email info@nihsa.gov.ng with subject line "Data Rights Request".

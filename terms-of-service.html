1. About the Application

NIHSA FloodWatch is an official digital service of the Nigeria Hydrological Services Agency (NIHSA), a parastatal under the Federal Ministry of Water Resources and Sanitation. The application provides:

  • Real-time river gauge readings and water level monitoring
  • Flood early warning alerts for Nigerian states and LGAs
  • Community flood report submission and verification
  • Hydrological forecasts and basin risk assessments
  • NIHSA AI — a hydrology-specialist AI assistant
  • Vanguard community coordination channels for trained flood responders
  • Evacuation centre locations and capacity information
